About This Website

Helping BYU-Idaho students discover fun, affordable activities around Rexburg.

Our Mission

This website was created to help students find free and low-cost adventures, food spots, entertainment, and events near BYU-Idaho.

Why We Made It

College students are always looking for fun things to do on a budget. This website makes it easy to explore Rexburg and nearby areas.

Student Community

Students can submit activities and help others discover hidden gems around Rexburg.
